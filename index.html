Frequently asked

How do I play? Open the in-app Rules screen from the home screen. Nutsy is a real-time race — everyone plays simultaneously, no turns.

Does the app collect my data? No accounts, no analytics, no ads. All progress is stored on your device only. See the Privacy Policy for details.

I found a bug. Email me with the device model, iOS version, and what you were doing when it happened.

About Nutsy

Nutsy is based on the classic card game Nertz (also known as Pounce, Racing Demon, or Squeal). Variants of the rules exist worldwide; this app uses one common ruleset documented in the in-app Rules screen.
