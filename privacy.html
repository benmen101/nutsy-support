Nutsy Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

This policy describes how the Nutsy mobile app handles your information. Nutsy is a single-developer indie card game; the policy is short on purpose.

What we collect

Nothing. The current version of Nutsy has no user accounts, no analytics SDK, no advertising network, and no third-party trackers. Everything that happens in the app stays on your device.

What stays on your device only

  • Your in-progress saved game (so you can resume after closing the app).
  • Your local stats: total games played, wins, win rate, high score. These never leave your phone.
  • Your settings (sound, haptics, text size, difficulty preference).

All of the above lives in app-private storage that other apps can't read. Uninstalling Nutsy removes it.

What we never collect

  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS).
  • No analytics or telemetry.
  • No location data, microphone, camera, contacts, or calendar access.
  • No crash reports.
  • No name, email, or password (the app has no sign-in flow).

Children

Nutsy is rated 4+ and appropriate for all ages. We don't collect any data from anyone, including children, because we don't collect data at all.

If a future version changes this

If a future version of Nutsy adds a feature that collects data — for example, online multiplayer with friends — this policy will be updated before the feature ships, and the in-app privacy screen will surface a notice describing what's new.
